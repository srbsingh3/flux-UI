Design Conversations

Flux is a fusion of unique skills, tools and ideologies in the ever evolving design industry. It is a platform for designers, artists and craftsmen to share their inspiring stories.

Chapter One

January 11, 2020 at Nutanix, Sarjapur Road

TALK

01

/ Stories Made of Paper

Hari & Deepti

Papercut Artists

Known internationally for their specific papercut and backlit style, Hari and Deepti a husband-wife artist duo moved to India from Denver in the fall of 2014.

They believe that “Paper is brutal in its simplicity as a medium. It demands the attention of the artist while it provides the softness they need to mold it in to something beautiful."

Know more: www.harianddeepti.com

TALK

02

/ Stories on the Wall

Raj & Harsh Kadam

Mural Painters

Raj and Harsh are a brother duo visual artists collaborating since their childhood to make artistic wonders.

Their work has a distinct artistic identity and is highly imaginative. Representing a unique amalgamation of their experience across Design, Visual Arts, Animation and Technology the duo looks at art as adventure.

Know more: www.inkbrushnme.com

TALK

03

/ Visual Narratives

Daan Botlek

Visual Storyteller

Street artist and illustrator Daan Botlek is based in Rotterdam, Netherlands and is known for his strange form of character-driven street art. Botlek uses surrealism, riddles, mathematics, isolation or humor to challenge his reality, but they somehow always follows a certain pattern. He questions the world throughout his artwork by creating paradox situations, which is also the purpose to those that engage.

Know more: www.daanbotlek.com
