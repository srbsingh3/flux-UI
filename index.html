03

/ Stories from Kochi

Tina & Pratheek

Visual Storytellers

Tina & Pratheek are writers, storytellers & the duo behind Kokaachi, a visual storytelling house based in Kochi.
They collaborate with illustrators and designers around the world to make a diverse range of graphic narratives. They have also directed and produced animation and title sequences for films like OK Kanmani, Lust Stories, Gully Boy and most recently, Ghost Stories.

Know more: www.kokaachi.com
